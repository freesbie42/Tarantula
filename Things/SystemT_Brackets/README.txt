# Summary

#### Brackets for TEVO Tarantula - Single or Dual Z-rod setups

Here are my remix of Rob Cad's brilliant brackets for TEVO Tarantula. This is a part of my complete remake of Tevo's printer. 

The manual with BOM and mount steps is available in the "Thing Files" section.
