# Summary

####Oldham coupler for TEVO Tarantula

Here's a remix of Doron Shalev's brilliant oldham coupler for the TEVO Tarantula. This device will help people get rid of the Z-wobbles. This is a part of my complete remake of Tevo's printer. 

The manual is available in the "Thing Files" section.
